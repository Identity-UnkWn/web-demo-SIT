About

Siddaganga Institute of Technology (SIT), situated in Tumkur, Karnataka, India, is a distinguished engineering college established in 1963. Affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University, SIT offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various engineering disciplines. The institution boasts state-of-the-art facilities, including modern laboratories and research centers, fostering innovation and academic excellence. SIT is renowned for its experienced faculty who impart quality education and mentor students effectively. The college emphasizes practical learning through industry collaborations and internships, ensuring students are well-prepared for professional challenges. Its alumni network spans globally, contributing significantly to diverse sectors. Notable features of SIT Tumkur include:

  • Established in 1963
  • Affiliated with Visvesvaraya Technological University
  • Offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs
  • State-of-the-art facilities and laboratories
  • Emphasis on innovation and research
  • Experienced faculty members
  • Industry collaborations and internships
  • Strong focus on practical learning
  • Globally connected alumni network
  • Contributes significantly to various sectors
